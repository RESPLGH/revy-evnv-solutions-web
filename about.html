About

Company

REVY Environmental Solutions has answers to all that take place in every step of the waste management and has proven competency in transforming waste into re-usable resources. Waste treatment and bio-methane production through Anaerobic Digestion (AD) process is our core area of expertise.

Our Mission

REVY's mission is to strive for innovation and provide economically feasible, eco-friendly & sustainable solutions to their customers.

Our Vision

The Vision of the company is to encourage better waste management across the country by providing innovative technological solutions for Effective treatment of waste/wastewater along with Energy and Nutrients to keep India clean and Energy surplus in a sustainable manner.
